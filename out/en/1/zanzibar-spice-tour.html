Zanzibar Spice Tour

The history of Zanzibar would be incomplete without mentioning cloves, nutmeg, cinnamon, pepper, and other spices introduced by the Sultan of Oman during the famous slave trade. This guided tour of the spice farms just outside Zanzibar City takes you deep into the island's hinterland to discover its rich tradition and culture. You will visit local villages, where you’ll experience the authentic daily life of the island and observe the famous wood and water carvers, crafting beautiful wooden objects. The tour continues through spice and fruit plantations, ending with a visit to the vibrant spice market.

Highlights

  • Discover Zanzibar's Spice Heritage: Learn about Zanzibar's spice trade history as you explore farms growing cloves, cinnamon, nutmeg, and pepper.
  • Local Villages Experience: Visit local villages and immerse yourself in Zanzibar's authentic culture, witnessing traditional lifestyles and crafts.
  • Wood and Water Carvers: Observe skilled artisans creating wooden crafts using traditional methods, a unique insight into Zanzibar's craftsmanship.
  • Spice and Fruit Plantations: Explore lush plantations filled with fragrant spices and tropical fruits, learning how they are cultivated and used.
  • Spice Market Visit: Conclude the tour at the bustling spice market, where you can see, smell, and purchase the spices that have shaped Zanzibar's history.

What To Expect

  • Expert Guide: An experienced guide will provide fascinating insights into Zanzibar's spice heritage, culture, and history.
  • Visit to Local Artisans: Observe local wood and water carvers at work, gaining an understanding of Zanzibar's unique artistic traditions.
  • Spice Farm Tour: Enjoy a guided walk through spice plantations where you can see, touch, and even taste a variety of spices and fruits.
  • Cultural Immersion: Engage with the local community as you visit villages and learn about traditional Zanzibari customs and daily life.
  • Round-Trip Transfers: Convenient round-trip transfers from your hotel are included, ensuring a seamless and enjoyable experience.
